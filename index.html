Introduction

Hello! I'm Alejandro U. Alvarez, an engineering student at Universidad de Oviedo (Spain). I started programming during my Freshman year in Brookline High School (Boston, MA), and then got more and more interested in web development. I'm currently involved in many projects, most of them related to social networking. I mainly develop the database schemas and back-end systems. I was inspired to redesign my website by my friend Qiming Fang

I love new ideas, trying new tools and working with new people. When it comes to work, I love git and apps like Teambox. I currently work with my friend Jaime Caballero in our little startup nuo studio

Spanish social network fully integrated with Facebook and Twitter. It alows users to express their opinion on their friends.
