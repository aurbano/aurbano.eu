Second time here, now with several more festivals under the belt and a better understanding of what it's all about! The first time we literally didn't talk with anyone with how overwhelming it felt!

But that's what makes the festivals special, the people. This time we made an effort to get to know people, to participate, to be present. And it changed everything. Each person we met had a story that I then tried to represent in the photos as best as I could.

This was when I discovered what photography meant to me, and what I wanted to say through it.
